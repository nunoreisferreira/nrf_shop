Chess is a strategic board game played between two players.

The main objective of the game is to checkmate the opponent's king, meaning placing it under attack in such a way that it cannot make a legal move to escape.

Chess is considered a combination of art, science, and sport, widely recognized for its tactical and strategic complexity.

Besides being a leisure activity, chess is also an educational tool, promoting the development of skills such as logical thinking, concentration, and problem-solving.
